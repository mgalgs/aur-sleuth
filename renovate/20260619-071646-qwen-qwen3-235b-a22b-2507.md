The script silently runs `sudo chmod` without prompting the user or justifying superuser privileges, increasing the risk of privilege escalation or abuse if the script is tampered with or executed unintentionally.

This is a classic supply-chain risk: the dependencies are not pinned, verified, or audited by Arch packaging standards.
